Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I apply for a credit card?

    You can apply for a credit card online, by phone, or by mail. The application process typically involves providing personal information, such as your name, address, and social security number.

  • What is a credit limit?

    A credit limit is the maximum amount of credit that a card issuer allows you to borrow on your credit card account.

  • How do I make a payment on my credit card?

    You can make a payment on your credit card online, by mail, or by phone. Most credit card issuers also offer automatic payment options to help you avoid late fees.
