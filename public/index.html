Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button in the header and follow the prompts to enter your email address and create a password.

How do I place an order?

To place an order, browse our shop and add items to your cart. Once you're ready to checkout, click on the cart icon in the header and follow the prompts to enter your shipping and payment information.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all purchases. If you're not satisfied with your product, simply contact us within 30 days of receiving it and we'll arrange for a return.
