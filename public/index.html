Frequently Asked Questions

  • How long does delivery take?

    Delivery times vary depending on your location and the time of day. Please allow 2-3 hours for delivery.

  • What if I'm not home when my order arrives?

    You can leave special instructions for the delivery driver in the checkout process. We'll make sure your groceries are safely delivered.

  • Can I cancel or modify my order?

    Yes, you can cancel or modify your order up until the delivery driver arrives. Please contact customer support for assistance.
