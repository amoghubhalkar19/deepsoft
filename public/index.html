Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is a credit card?

    A credit card is a payment card issued to users as a means to borrow money before buying goods and services, based on the holder's promise to pay for them.

  • How does a credit card work?

    When you make a purchase with a credit card, the merchant receives payment from the credit card company, and you are then responsible for paying back the credit card company.

  • What are the benefits of using a credit card?

    Credit cards offer convenience, rewards programs, and the ability to build credit history.
