Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: How does the buying process work?

    A: Once you find a car you're interested in, you can contact the seller directly through our platform to arrange a test drive or inspection.

  • Q: Is it safe to buy a car from a stranger?

    A: We ensure secure transactions through our trusted payment gateway and escrow service, which holds the funds until the car has been delivered and inspected by the buyer.
